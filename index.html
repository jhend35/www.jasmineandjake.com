Groomsmen

Joshua Henderson Best Man, Brother of the Groom

Josh is my older brother and my best friend. Luck of the draw I know, but he's always had my back and been there when I needed him. Our mutual interst in sports always gives us way too much stuff to talk about. I couldn't ask for a better person to be my Best Man.

If you ask Josh or I who is better at anything we will both answer, "I am." He is lying.

Al Palmiano Groomsman, Brother of the Bride

Al is definately the planner of the Palmiano family. Everytime we go somewhere, he knows the best places to eat, drink, and have a good time. It's never a boring time when he is around, and I am lucky to have him as a brother-in-law.

Andrew Lato Groomsman

Andrew has been my best friend since I could walk. And while we didnt end up going to the same high school or college, we still managed to find a way to always hang out. From homemade Jackass movies to late night video games, I know it would have not been the same with Lato.

Austin Mayeux Groomsman

Austin and I have been friends since middle school. I give him 1/4 the blame for me losing all my athletic ability in high school while we were doing awesomely stupid stuff on false river. In college, we tore up the vaunted LSU golf course where he witnessed my greatest round of golf.

Bryant PalmianoGroomsman, Brother of the Bride

Bryant is the middle sibling in the Palmiano Family. He is a genius, loves music, and refuses to fit any mold. Our conversations usually turn to technology and computers, a shared interest, and I am lucky to have him as a brother-in-law.

Chris deCesare Groomsman

In my entire life I have not met one person who didn't laugh at Chris. You won't meet someone more outgoing or enthusiastic. He's the kind of guy who will put you in a better mood just by being around. We've been friends 15 years, and he's been that way since I met him. I consider myself fortunate to have him as a friend.

Daniel Gilbert Groomsman

Daniel and I grew close in college when we were the only ones who would rather chill and play video games than go out to Tigerland and get stupid. Plus, it didnt hurt that every 5 minutes we were brainstorming a new idea to work on.

Derek Gilbert Groomsman

Derek and I also grew close in college when Sorority Boot Camp followed by MickeyD's breakfast became our full time job and first business. Also, without his now wife being roommates with Jasmine at LSU, I would have never met Jasmine.

Bridesmaids

Ann Catherine Mouton Maid of Honor, Bride's Best Friend

With Jasmine having two brothers, Ann Catherine, along with the other bridesmaids, is the closest thing Jasmine has as a sister. Jasmine and Ann Catherine met at St. Edward’s, grew up together (two streets away), and, now, live four doors down from each other. Jasmine can always count on Ann Catherine to be there for any adventure either of them come up with and was a enormous help when Jake was planning the proposal of a lifetime.

Ashley SchroederBridesmaid, Groom's Sister

Ashley is the most generous, fun, considerate person, and Jasmine is lucky enough to have her as a a future sister-in-law. Anytime you ask Jake about a favorite memory growing up, Ashley was always there. It is only fitting for Ashley to be a huge part of the wedding.

Crystal De RosalesBridesmaid, Bride's Cousin

During Jasmine’s first trips to California, Crystal would take Jasmine's hand and lead the way to show her everything. Even today, the same story still remains true. Crystal is always sure to show a good time when Jasmine & Jake visit California. Crystal is a clinical laboratory scientist, which means she’s super logical and smart… Jasmine wouldn’t want antibody else to be standing next to her (see what I did there?), keeping her cool on the wedding day.

Kassie RoyBridesmaid, Bride's Best Friend

Jasmine met Kassie during middle school and it didn't take too long for them to become friends. Anytime Jasmine wants a pick me up or to get a party started, she turns to Kassie. There is always guaranteed laughter (and possibly a few shots) when Jasmine and Kassie are together. As a chemical engineer and book lover, Kassie is passing on her smarts to her son and soon-to-be born daughter, Decker and Calla, and Jasmine could not be prouder.

Mallary LouviereBridesmaid, Bride's Best Friend

Mallary is the epitome of being laid back, fun-loving, and care free. She is the reason Jasmine knows how to just let it go, have fun, and sing along with the music (how does she know every word of every rap song?!). Mallary was the friend who “won” the Prenatal Spa Day Raffle and was able to answer all of the many questions Jasmine had without any hesitation - just goes to show how wonderful of a mother she is! 

Maria Palmiano Bridesmaid, Bride's Sister-in-Law

Maria is the girl who keeps Jasmine’s oldest brother, Al, in line (THANK GOD). When Al first introduced Maria to the family, we knew it was fate. She is so very patient, a great listener, and gives the best advice. Even more exciting, they are soon-to-be parents of Jasmine’s first official niece, Emily.

Noelle ComeauxBridesmaid, Bride's Best Friend

Call her Nell, Nellie, Nells, ElleC, Nella. This girl has been there for Jasmine through everything (they also met while at St. Edward's)- through elementary, high school, college, even being sorority sisters, to now… she is Jasmine's absolute greatest friend. Jasmine looks up to Noelle's creativity, organization, down-to-earth attitude, and kind heart.

Verna Jane AbanteBridesmaid, Bride's Cousin

If you haven’t met Verna Jane yet, you need to. She is a quadruple threat - beautiful, smart, always so very thoughtful, and hilarious. She makes everything more fun, and Jasmine can always count on her to roll the windows down and jam Whitney Houston songs with her. Verna Jane is one of those people that makes you want to sing, laugh, dance, run, explore, play, and cry - all in the same day (and she’ll do all of those things with you).
